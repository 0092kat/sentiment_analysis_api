About Sentiment Analysis App

Welcome to the Sentiment Analysis App! This web application is designed to analyze the sentiment of text inputs and provide you with insights into the emotional tone of the text.

How It Works

To analyze sentiment, simply enter a piece of text in the input box on the home page and click the "Analyze Sentiment" button. The app will evaluate the text and classify it as positive, negative, or neutral.

Use Cases

Our Sentiment Analysis App can be useful for a variety of purposes, including:

  • Understanding customer reviews
  • Understanding customer feedback

Privacy and Data Usage

We respect your privacy, and no data entered into the app is stored or shared. All text inputs are processed solely for sentiment analysis purposes.
